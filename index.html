"Improving personal safety in an intuitive and accessible way."

GuardianLive is an application designed to increase individual's safety in any given situation. We achieve this by providing users with tools to ensure that if something goes wrong, they are able to get help as quickly and easily as possible.

Powerful Safety Features

Smart Safety Countdowns

Set customizable timers that automatically alert your emergency contacts if you don't check in on time. Perfect for any situation where you want an extra layer of security.

Live Video Streaming

Broadcast your surroundings in real-time to your contacts. VOD recordings are stored on the cloud and accessible to you and your contacts at any time.

Emergency Network

Add trusted friends and family who will be automatically notified when you need help. They'll receive your live location and stream access instantly.

Auto-Alert System

If your countdown expires without a response, GuardianLive automatically notifies your contacts with your location and any active streams.

Duress PIN System

A sophisticated two-PIN system: one confirms you're safe, while the other silently signals you're in danger while appearing completely normal.

The Duress System

Silent Protection When You Need It Most

The Emergency PIN (Duress PIN) is a powerful safety feature that lets you signal danger while appearing completely normal to anyone watching. It's designed for situations where you can't openly call for help.

Double PIN Security

Normal PIN: Confirms you're safe. Notifies contacts everything is OK.

Emergency PIN: Silently alerts contacts while appearing to complete the functionality normally.

1

Start Activity

Begin a timer or stream. Contacts see your location.

2

Enter Emergency PIN

If you are under duress, enter the Emergency PIN.

3

Silent Alert

Contacts are notified instantly with a DURESS badge.

4

Help Arrives

Contacts view your live location and take action.

Timer Flow

  • 1. Start Timer: You're tracked normally.
  • 2. Enter Duress PIN: Someone forces you to stop the timer. You enter the silent PIN.
  • 3. Appear Normal: The app looks like it stopped normally. No alarm sounds.
  • 4. Silent Alert: Contacts are notified immediately that you are under duress.

Stream Flow

  • 1. Go Live: Video is saved to cloud.
  • 2. Enter Duress PIN: The stream ends normally but triggers the alert.
  • 3. Evidence Saved: Contacts get the recording link and your live location.
  • 4. Action Taken: Your location is tracked until you are marked safe.

What your contacts see:

A red DURESS badge on your profile, your full location history since the activity started, and the VOD recording if you were streaming. They are empowered to call you or emergency services with full context.
