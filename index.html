  • You control who's alerted. You can directly select which contacts will be notified
  • Status at a glance. Any contact who's running a safety timer is highlighted and pulled to the top, so you can see who's currently out and counting down at a glance

Real-Time Tracking

Stay connected until you're safe

While your timer is active, your contacts can open it at any time to see how you're doing, in case they need to act before the timer expires.

  • Time remaining and timer details are visible to your contacts in real time
  • Live location map showing your path from start to current position
  • Want to be discreet? When setting up a timer, enable the "Hidden until expiry" option to stay invisible to your contacts unless your timer actually expires
Automatic Alerts

Contacts immediately see expired timers and their details

No vague "check on your friend" notifications. The moment your timer runs out, you're flagged At Risk and your contacts get the exact information they need to act.

  • Flagged instantly. You move to the top of their list, marked At Risk
  • How to respond? Your personal instructions, a map of your last known location, and the live stream (or its saved recording, if one was started), are all immediately available to your contacts
  • Accidental expiry? No problem! Just mark yourself as safe with a message saying that you're okay, it was an accident.
Emergency PIN System

Silent protection under pressure

Two PINs that look identical in use. One PIN confirms you're safe; the emergency PIN appears to work normally, while silently alerting your selected contacts that you are in danger. Meant to be used if you are under duress.

  • The emergency PIN looks completely normal to anyone watching over your shoulder
  • Contacts see a DURESS badge with your live location the moment you enter the emergency PIN
  • Works everywhere in the app. Stopping timers, ending streams, and deleting history records are all PIN protected

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first timer.

What happens if my timer expires and I'm actually fine?

You stay in control the whole time. You can extend the timer mid-activity with Modify Time, or stop it the moment you're done by entering your PIN. If it does expire by accident, just open the app and mark yourself safe with a message saying it was an accident. Your contacts see the update immediately.

What if my phone dies or loses signal?

Your timer runs on our servers, not just on your phone. If the timer expires and you haven't checked in for any reason, including a dead battery or loss of signal, your emergency contacts are still alerted with your last known location.

Who can see my location?

Only the emergency contacts you've personally selected to alert for that timer, and only while a timer or stream is active. GuardianLive is not a constant tracker. When no activity is running, nothing is tracked or shared.

Do my emergency contacts need the app?

Yes, your contacts use GuardianLive to see your active timers, watch live streams, and receive alerts with your location and response instructions. Adding each other takes seconds.

Will it drain my battery?

GuardianLive only shares your location while a timer or stream is active, so there's no constant background tracking eating your battery. Live video streaming uses more power, like any camera app, but a plain timer is just a countdown with occasional location updates, so it barely touches your battery.

How does the emergency PIN work?

You set two PINs that look identical in use. Your normal PIN confirms you're safe. Your emergency PIN appears to work exactly the same way but silently alerts your contacts with a DURESS badge and your live location. Anyone watching over your shoulder will not know that you sent a distress signal.
